The Best Movies

Top 3 movies of all-time.

Nausicaä

A devastating war, which only a few civilizations have survived, has turned the earth into a desolate wasteland on which a fungus forest is spreading, the spores of which are highly toxic to humans.

Fight Club

An office worker suffering from insomnia looks for a way to turn his life around and meets a carefree soap merchant who runs an underground fight club that turns out to be something even bigger.

Old Boy

After being kidnapped and imprisoned for 15 years, Oh Dae-su is suddenly released, only to be told that he must find his kidnapper in 5 days.
